Ca Coast

July, 2020

David and I drove from NorCal to SoCal, stayed at the Treehouse for a few weeks, and then drove from SoCal to NorCal. But, lots of driving equals lots of quality time equals lots of annoyance equals both of us going insane. Just kidding! It was fun. On the drive down we stopped in Santa Barbara and camped at Midland, in the Santa Barbara Mountains. I went on the hardest hike of my life (actually, I was tearing up by the end), and ate some of the best food of my life (dayum good). On the way up we drove along Highway 1, but not without our best deceases friend, Arnold Palmer!
