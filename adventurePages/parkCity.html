Park City

September, 2020

I've lived in California my whole life. A month in Utah opened my eyes. I have fallen in love with the Wasatch Mountains. The canyons breathe silence yet take my breath away. The lakes and the peaks and red rocks below – Utah, you amaze me. We were there as the trees changed colors, from green to yellow to red to bare. I even ran 11 miles one day, motivated by the new beauty I passed with every step. And boy oh boy, I never would have guessed that I would be living on my own with three boys. But boy oh boy it was so fun! What a journey this was, and what an experience we shared, from buying 60 eggs at a time, to driving an extra hour to try to find but not find Alex's wallet, to going on a random five-mile hike and running into a moose. Happy happy happy!
